How it works
In order to build a base of financial support I am vested in gaining monthly candle subscribers. Each month I will send out a Mandle that is themed for that month. For example in October or November you might receive a orange candle with a pumpkin or warm chai essential oil fragrance, in Winter you might get a white or blue color with spruce or pine scent. The idea is my Mandles are not just a candle that burns well and smells great, but something you will feel proud to be apart of your home decor. It would be a great table centerpiece or fit right in on the bathroom sink counter or living room coffee table. To subscribe to my monthly Mandles you can look for the package that fits your desired giving level or needs and fill out the form attached. Directions on shipping and payment will be included.
